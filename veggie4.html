Vegetable Burrito Bowl

This vegetable burrito bowl is a delicious and healthy meal that's perfect for lunch or dinner. It's packed with flavorful ingredients and is easy to customize with your favorite toppings.

To make this vegetable burrito bowl, you'll need:

  • 1 cup cooked rice (white or brown)
  • 1 cup black beans, drained and rinsed
  • 1 cup corn kernels (fresh, canned, or frozen)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 cup diced red onion
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
  • Salsa, sour cream, or guacamole for topping (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Divide the cooked rice among serving bowls.
  2. Top each bowl with black beans, corn kernels, cherry tomatoes, avocado slices, diced red onion, and chopped cilantro.
  3. Squeeze fresh lime juice over the bowls.
  4. Optional: Serve with salsa, sour cream, or guacamole on the side.
  5. Enjoy your delicious vegetable burrito bowls!
