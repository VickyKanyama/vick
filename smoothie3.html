Strawberry Lemonade

This refreshing strawberry lemonade is perfect for hot summer days. Made with fresh strawberries, tangy lemon juice, and a hint of sweetness, it's sure to be a hit with everyone!

To make this strawberry lemonade, you'll need:

  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, hulled and halved
  • 1/2 cup fresh lemon juice (about 3-4 lemons)
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 4 cups cold water
  • Ice cubes
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine the fresh strawberries, lemon juice, and granulated sugar. Blend until smooth.
  2. Strain the strawberry mixture through a fine mesh sieve into a pitcher to remove any seeds or pulp.
  3. Add cold water to the pitcher and stir to combine.
  4. Chill the strawberry lemonade in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving.
  5. Fill glasses with ice cubes and pour the chilled strawberry lemonade over the ice.
  6. Garnish with fresh mint leaves, if desired.
  7. Enjoy your delicious strawberry lemonade!
