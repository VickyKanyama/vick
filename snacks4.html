Fruity Splash Smoothie

This fruity splash smoothie is bursting with flavor and packed with nutrients. Made with a blend of tropical fruits and creamy yogurt, it's the perfect way to start your day or refuel after a workout.

To make this fruity splash smoothie, you'll need:

  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries
  • 1/2 cup frozen pineapple chunks
  • 1/2 cup frozen mango chunks
  • 1 ripe banana, peeled and sliced
  • 1 cup plain yogurt (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1 tablespoon honey (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Place all the frozen fruits, sliced banana, yogurt, orange juice, and honey (if using) in a blender.
  2. Blend on high speed until smooth and creamy, adding more orange juice if needed to reach your desired consistency.
  3. Pour the smoothie into glasses and serve immediately.
  4. Enjoy your refreshing and delicious fruity splash smoothie!
