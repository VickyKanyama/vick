Vegetable Stir-Fry

This vegetable stir-fry is a quick and easy dish that's perfect for busy weeknights. Packed with colorful vegetables and tossed in a savory sauce, it's both healthy and delicious.

To make this stir-fry, you'll need:

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (such as bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, and snap peas)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, grated
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, sesame oil, garlic, ginger, cornstarch, and water to make the sauce.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Add mixed vegetables to the skillet and cook until crisp-tender, about 5 minutes.
  4. Pour the sauce over the vegetables and stir to coat.
  5. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, until the sauce has thickened and vegetables are cooked through.
  6. Remove from heat and serve immediately. Enjoy!
